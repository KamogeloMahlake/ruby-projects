“Has Sensei finally gone over the edge?” She asked as she stepped under the gates of the village which she had vowed never to return.

Kakashi Hatake, the son of the White Fang, Sakumo Hatake and the student of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze.

It was the ones who could remain in this profession and still keep their sanity who were the odd ones.

After shock came anger.

“Konoha is on the verge of a Civil War and I need your help to stop it.”

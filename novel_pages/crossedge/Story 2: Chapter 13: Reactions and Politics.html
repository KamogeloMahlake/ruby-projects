Unlike yesterday, today was something I was looking forward to. The official start of my business.


Four of my stores will be opened in the capital cities of the four Satans today.


Last night, all of us had already arranged the last batch of stocks for all four stores and all that was needed was to teleport them.


The time of opening was 9 am and we still had four hours on our hands.


“I will be taking these to the Asmodeus and Sitri territories. When the shops are stocked, I will come to New Lilith for the opening ceremony.” Armin said as he and his peerage members carried all the items to be teleported.


“Alright. I will escort the goods to the Lucifer capital and Souji-san will bring the stock for the Beelzebub capital. See you at New Lilith.”


With that said, Armin teleported away with his peerage members.


I too got to work.


My peerage members had already arranged the things so we teleported.


Latia and Rias had returned back to their homes early on and will be arriving with their families for the opening ceremony.


I arrived at the storeroom which I had purchased a while ago. There were already several people working there since early Morning.


“My Lord.” I heard a voice and looked towards the direction to see a man walking towards me.


“Just call me Mathias, uncle.” I smiled.


He was Logan Buné. He was the father of Ladora Buné and a distant relative of mine. The guy had fallen in love with a human magician, one of his contractors and thus my cousin Ladora was born. The man never regretted his decision despite his hard life since then.


My parents had often helped him and he had offered to take me in. I didn’t accept their help because, first, I had the support and protection of Sirzechs and Tiamat and second, I didn’t want to burden them. So now that I am the Head of their House, I can openly help them out.


Ladora was older than me and extremely hardworking.


I had often sent him my special crops although I never told them anything openly.


Recently, I disclosed everything to them and invited them to stay in the Buné territories and help me in my business.


Safe to say that he was quite enthusiastic.


I had made him the manager of the store at New Lilith while Ladora looked after the store in the Beelzebub territory.


They even had contracted two of my dragons as familiars for the sake of protection. I will be working to increase their strengths to the Ultimate Class in a few years.


“How is the work going on, Uncle?”


“Everything is progressing smoothly. The shop has already been stocked. With the things you have brought, even the Restaurant’s stock will be full. We will be ready to open any time.” he reported


“That’s great. How about the staff?” I asked.


“I contacted everyone I knew, who had followed our house for a long time and didn’t leave the territory despite the harsh conditions. They have been loyal to the Buné House since the time of your Grandfather. All of them responded. All of the staff were selected from among these people.” he replied as he led me to the shop.


The shop at New Lilith was an eleven storied building.


I had bought this building through Septus and Latia took care of the renovation.


The first four floors of the building was the store, each floor divided by the Rank of products, with the lowest floor being for F Ranked ones, then E Ranked and then D Ranked ones.


Above it was the floor where people could order as well as receive C Ranked products.


The floors above those were for the Restaurant, one separate floor for the Low Class, Mid Class, High Class Devils and the VIP Floor.


Above that was a bar where different alcohols from my farm were sold.


The tenth floor was for the auction of the B Ranked Products and the top floor was the office for staff.


The warehouse was in the basement floors of the building.


I had fortified the entire place from top till the bottom with my best wards. This building was as protected as the Lucifer Castle in the same city and that was saying something.


There was a very low chance that someone would try to attack it in the Lucifer capital but why take any chances?


As for the name of the Restaurant… Hotel Gourmet.


I decided to create a Hotel Gourmet of my own in this world.


The staff was running around in a hurry, getting things ready at the last minute.


They were getting a thirty percent discount on a certain amount of products till the D Rank so they were happy.


Time passed.


I had sent invitations to every house as well as my allies for the opening of my first store at New Lilith.


This time, I wanted to see how many of them will appear.


- Venelana Gremory -


She waited for the arrival of Zekram as she drank her tea.


Zeoticus was sitting beside him and her mother-in-law and the Gremory ancestor was sitting in front of them.


Runeas Gremory was eating her favourite pudding with great enthusiasm.


“You seem to enjoy this sweet a lot, ancestor.” her mother-in-law asked with a smile.


“Umm. Crusty makes amazing puddings.” She smiled, taking another spoonful of the dessert.


Crusty was the name of the Pudding Camel which she had gotten as a gift from Mathias and she agreed, it really made amazing puddings.


“If you like those then I think you will enjoy today’s party even more. You couldn’t see it the last time but Mathias has a tree which grows different confectioneries seasons. You can get as many sweets as you like there.”


Runeas stopped eating and looked at them in surprise. “Seriously?! A confectionery tree? That boy, just what goes on in that head of his? Even Zagan wasn’t that crazy. But then again he was crazy in a different way. Instead of plants and animals which could be eaten by people, he enjoyed creating plants and animals who enjoyed eating people. In comparison, the boy is quite sane.”


This got them curious.


“Ancestor.” Zeoticus asked, interested in the sudden topic.


“Hmm?”


“Just what kind of man was the original Zagan?”


“Zagan? He was…”


“... A shut in mad scientist.” The answer came from a different direction.


They looked in the direction of the voice and saw the man they had been waiting for coming.


Zeoticus walked forward and received him as she and her mother-in-law stood up and bowed as well.


He nodded but his eyes didn’t leave Runeas the whole time.


“You look old.” Runeas said, looking up from her pudding before diving back into it.


They sweatdropped.


“And you look just as bratty as before.” He smiled before taking a seat.


“Hmph. See, you are even talking like an old man now.”


“Well, I am old.” He shrugged.


They kept quiet, watching their conversation from the side.


“I assume you woke up because of the Zagan boy this time?” He asked, directly getting to the topic. He looked at them. “That brat Sirzechs pulled a fast one on all of us. He’s learning things now.”


“Yup. It was quite smart.” Runeas replied nonchalantly.


“And it has caused quite a ruckus all over the Underworld.”


“Right! It was hilarious watching him fight with a hoe. That chicken brat didn’t stand a chance hahahaha.”


….


Zekram rolled his eyes at her antics. “So… did you meet him?”


“Hmm. I even got specially made camels from him.”


“Oh! Did he recognise you?” He asked. “Knowing you, there is no way you didn’t try to trick him.”


“Tch. That boy is too smart for his own good. That Buné aspect of his identified me. He even sensed that my Demonic Power was extremely old. Seriously, even Zagan couldn’t do it. I know, I have pulled many pranks on him and he never found out. At least he knows how to please others. The camels I got from him are the new gems of my collection.” She pushed a plate of pudding towards him and summoned a bottle of wine.


Zekram poured the wine for himself and took a sip.


“Hmm, this is quite good. I can see why the boy was so confident in his products.”


“Hmph, that wine comes from my special Wine Camels. Instead of milk, they produce high quality wine.” She replied.


“Produce wine?”


“How would I know? Zagans and their crazy brains. He apparently has a tree of confectioneries on his farm too.”


Zekram had a strange look on his face. “Yes. Zagans were always weird. Strange but talented nonetheless. After all, there was a reason Lord Lucifer wanted to name him as the second Great King of the Underworld. The Great King of Creation, mirroring my own self.”


What!


Their eyes widened at the sudden reveal of the information.


“Yeah. If only Beelzebub hadn’t been a prick back then. I mean what reason was there for him to be jealous of Zagan because he could make more kinds of flies than him? Even little kids aren’t that petty.”


….


“Only they knew what went on in their heads. From your words it seems that you like him.”


“Hmm.”


“I guess I will have to invite him over for tea sometime. I would have already if I had been informed earlier.” He looked at them and then back towards his wine.


“It was for the safety of Mathias. Even we were not told anything about him.” Her mother-in-law defended.


“Oh drop it. You know those fools on the council would have chewed the boy out before he could even grow. Not to mention that mess with Naberius. Seriously, what’s wrong with these fools? If you are experimenting on people, do it to other races. There are still angels and fallen angels alive in this world. Why go after our own?” Runeas complained.


“Just because they call themselves the Great King faction doesn’t mean they are all controlled by me. Most of the time, I don’t even know what they do. And I don’t interfere as long as they don’t go too far. That’s how children grow after all. What Naberius did had crossed the line and thus the entire house is currently suffering. I didn’t defend him.” Zekram shrugged, pouring himself another cup.


“Hmm. Isn’t that because you are lazy?” Runeas joked.


“Says the one who sleeps for thousands of years.”


“Touché.” She smiled.


“I have heard that you have engaged your daughter to him.” he turned towards her and asked.


She nodded. “They get along very well.”


“Good work. Bind him with your family. From what little I have heard, his talents could bring about a great change in the Underworld. Make sure you keep him attached to the devil race and the current government as much as possible.”


She couldn’t resist raising an eyebrow at this.


Zekram saw her reaction and rolled his eyes. “Just what kind of monster do you think I am? I am already retired. If there’s something I care about right now, it's the progress and development of the Devil Race and that boy’s talents have the potential to transform the entire society into something greater, just like Ajuka. I am not a fool to ruin or distance such a talent… well, unless he stands on the opposite side of the Underworld. After all, I have seen the true horror of Zagan with my own eyes. I would not let someone even more talented than him turn against the underworld.”


“Mathias has no such intentions. In fact, he is a very friendly boy who doesn’t even involve himself with politics. He enjoys working on his farm without disturbance. Moreover, he is extremely close to Rias and Latia as well as his friends.”


“A shut-in just like his ancestor.” Runeas added. “But yes. He’s safe. Sirzechs and the rest ran several psych tests on him since his childhood. Actually, we were going to the opening ceremony of his shop. Why don’t you join us too? There will be far better food and wine than this stuff. The boy keeps the best things stuffed in a separate collection and I plan to drink him dry today.”


“Oh. I did hear about the shop. Very well, since that’s the case, I will join. Let’s see if the food craze of the Zagan is really worth its name.”

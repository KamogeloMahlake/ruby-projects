The killing intent the two figures were exuding was making even him experience chills just by standing in front of them.


The shop owner and his daughter looked pale at the sight of the two.


They were thinking about what to do when the white haired girl got up from her seat and walked casually to his side and smacked his head lightly. “Calm down, stupid Naruto. You’re scaring the shopkeeper. I want more ramen.”


“Ow… I… I am sorry, Kanna-chan.” Naruto apologised before looking at the two figures in front of him. “It’s okay, mom, dad. I am not in danger. I was just… angry. You can rest.”


To their utter surprise, the figures slowly faded away.


Naruto then went on to apologise to the shop owner and his daughter and then looked at the white haired girl, Kanna. “Let’s go. We are getting late.”


The girl nodded.


“Naruto. Let me explain everything. It’s not what you think.” Sensei went forward but stayed a few feet away from him, not risking what had just happened.


“I don’t want to talk to you. I am just here to take a picture of my parents and I will leave after that.” The boy answered curtly.


“I have their pictures. In fact, I have all their belongings too. Your father wanted those to be given to you when you were older. Come with me. Let me explain everything first. You can take whatever you want after that.” Sensei tried to explain.


The kid however just narrowed his eyes at his words.


“He is not lying, kid. Your parents’ belongings are indeed with him. At least hear him out once. Your friend can also come along if you want.” Jiraiya also went ahead and added carefully.


The kid looked at the girl who nodded. “Let’s go. They are weak. If they try something, I will beat them up.” She patted her chest confidently.


The scene made Jiraiya smile even with all the crap that was going on.


“Are you sure, Kanna-chan?”


“Hmm.”


“Okay then.” He then looked at them and nodded. He created a shadow clone to take care of the situation there while his original body left with the rest of the group.


They turned towards the Hokage building and the white haired girl grew wings and picked Naruto up, following them, leaving a stunned audience around.


_________________________________x_________________________________


They entered the hokage’s office through the window and Naruto and the girl landed behind them as her wings shrunk back into her back.


Jiraiya watched in awe and couldn’t help but ask. “What kind of jutsu is that?”


The girl looked at him oddly and it was Naruto who answered. “Kanna-chan is a dragon. Those are her real wings. She hides them when she is in human form.”


“A what?”


“A Dragon.”


And as if to smack that fact into their heads, the girl grew horns and a tail too.


Jiraiya watched closely and indeed they were growing out of her body.


Was she a summoning beast?


He had once heard Orochimaru say that the snakes in the Ryuichi Cave could take the form of humans too.


But Dragons?


Did Dragons even exist?


He had never heard of any such summoning beast. Not even the toads of Mt. Myoboku knew of them.


And had he somehow reverse summoned himself in the summoning land of dragons? There was an example of this already… Jiraiya himself had done something like that.


It would also match up with his increased growth. From what he had known Naruto wasn’t exactly good in his classes and yet he had apparently effortlessly defeated the top student of his class after returning. So the training he was getting there must have been good.


He exchanged a look with sense who was listening to the conversation from the side and asked further.


“Oh… were you living with her… I mean dragons?”


The boy narrowed his eyes suspiciously at him. Geez, just how much did sensei mess up.


Jiraiya raised his hand quickly, trying to calm them down. “No need to get mad. I am just curious. I have never heard of any dragons before and wanted to know more about them. Where do they live? I have travelled all over the world but this is my first time seeing one.”


Naruto was about to speak but the girl stopped him and turned towards him.


“Why should I tell you? What’s in it for us?” She asked.


Finding a clue, Jiraiya dove in. “Oh, and what is it that you want?”


A glint passed by the girl’s eyes. “That bunshin thing you did back there… teach that to Naruto.” She asked and suddenly Naruto also had a look of realisation on his face and nodded.


“Bunshin… you mean the Kage Bunshin?” He asked, confused.


“Umm, the Kage Bunshin… tell me how to use it and I will tell you where the dragons live.” Naruto nodded.


“Well kid, it’s not an easy technique. It might take you a lot of time to learn that.” He answered, pretending to think.


“And you would need to complete your academy too. It's a jutsu only ninjas can learn. And didn't you want to be the Hokage, Naruto?” Sensei asked.


The question however had the opposite effect to what he was expecting. The frown on the boy's face returned with full force.


“I don’t want to be Hokage or even a ninja. I will become a Legendary Master like Gramps.”


Just what had those dragons been teaching him?


“But Naruto… your father… he was the fourth Hokage.  He was a great man who gave up his life to protect the village. Don’t you want to become like him?” Sensei asked, the worry in his voice, easy to recognise.


“And my father would also have wanted me to grow up properly and yet, I was thrown out of the orphanage, mocked and shunned by everyone.” The words were like a punch to their guts. “My parents gave up their lives to protect me, so that I can live a better life and I don’t think being a ninja is that life for me.”


“Who told you that, Naruto? Just who took you away from Konoha.” Sensei asked, his voice agitated slightly.


And as if to make things worse, the door opened and his two advisors entered… with the man he didn’t want to see in tow.


Behind him, Tsunade, Fugaku Uchiha and his sons also entered.


Jiraiya noticed the tension in the air between the two groups and the looks they threw at each other.


“Danzo… what are you doing here? I remember saying that you were not allowed anywhere outside your quarters.” Sensei asked in a warning tone.


“The boy has been found. I am here to prove that I wasn’t involved in any of this.” Danzo replied, unbothered. “But the more important question is where has he been all this time and how was he taken away? You do understand that letting such a significant military asset to the village get lost is your responsibility.”


“You do not… need to teach me what my responsibility is. I still haven’t permitted you to come out of your house arrest.”


“He is not, Hiruzen.” Koharu complained.


“Enough! You will keep quiet. That’s an order.” Sensei shouted, taking all of them by surprise. It was a side of sensei they had rarely seen before.


Apparently, someone else was getting annoyed by the whole situation too.


Naruto was the one who broke the silence. “Old man, I have waited long enough. When will you give me the pictures of my parents? I need to go back home.”


“This is your home, Naruto. Don’t you want to know about your parents? Jiraiya here, was your father’s sensei. He can tell you everything about them and even teach you ninjutsu like him.”


“Yes kid. Didn’t you want to learn the Kage Bunshin justu? I can  teach you that. In fact, your friend can join too. I am a summoner too and I know a lot about summoning beasts.”


“I don’t want any of that. I don’t want to be a ninja. I am going to be a Legendary Master dattebayo. I told you that already. Now, are you going to give me the pictures of my parents or not?”


The words brought out several reactions from the rest of the people.


“What does he mean by that, Hiruzen? You didn’t tell him… how could you? Do you have any idea what would happen if he spreads the identity…”


“And what is this about going back home? Where exactly is he going?”


“Letting a strategic resource of the village getaway is absolutely unacceptable.”


The situation was getting completely out of control as sensei and his advisors delved into an argument.


Naruto also seemed done with everything and looked at the girl.


“It seems they were lying to us again, Kanna-chan. Let’s go. We can find their pictures later.”


The girl however pointed towards a picture on the wall. “There… a picture of your dad.” Naruto looked back in surprise and his eyes lit up.


“Oh yeah.” He jumped towards the wall and removed Minato’s picture from there. “Now only the picture of my mom is left. We can totally find it by ourselves. You are awesome, Kanna-chan.”


The simple statement however brought several reactions, especially from the Uchiha who were staring wide eyed at the kid.


Aghh… this is turning into such a mess.


“Hmm. Let’s go.” The girl walked towards him.


“Where exactly are you going?” Sensei asked.


“Home.” Naruto replied as the girl sprouted wings and took hold of them and were about to fly.


“Stop, Naruto!” Sensei commanded and several anbu surrounded them in an instant, closing their exit.


“Sensei!” he called out but sensei raised his hand.


“Calm down, Jiraiya. He is just angry right now. We need to explain things to him first and know where he found everything from. Till then they need to be kept here.”


“Hey, let us go.” Naruto shouted while the white haired girl also let him down on the ground and glared at the anbu, almost as if preparing to attack when something happened which took them all by surprise once more.


Golden Chains erupted from Naruto’s back and pushed all the anbu away.


Few of them were thrown against the wall and the rest dodged, regaining their footing and drawing their weapons, surrounding him.


Those were… Kushina’s Adamantine Sealing Chains…


The tense atmosphere was broken by a new voice.


“What is going on here?”


They all looked towards the window where a girl was standing. She had long blonde hair with flaming orange tips and red-orange eyes with dilated pupils and was wearing a long blue dress over a white shirt and a hair band. The most surprising thing about here however were the horns and the green lizard-like tail poking out of the dress.


Behind her was another girl. This one appeared much younger in age. She had pink hair tied up in a twin tails, and was floating in the air.


“Tohru-sama.”


“Tohru-chan.”


“Naruto-kun, Kanna, what is going on here? Why did you two run away without informing anyone? Do you have any idea how much everyone is worried?”


“Umm sorry. I… we just came here to get the pictures of my mom and dad. I got one and need one more and when we were returning they stopped us.” He pointed towards the anbu surrounding them.


Sensei stepped forward. “Are you one of the contracted dragons of Naruto? I have never heard of any dragons. Where do you all live?”


The girl looked at him with narrowed eyes. “Dragons live in the world of dragons. Now, why are you stopping them from returning home?” She asked.


“This place is Naruto’s home. He needs to spend time here too. I can understand that you want him to train in your arts but he needs to bond with the people of the village too.”


“We already know what kind of home this village was to Naruto-kun.” She replied with narrowed eyes. “This place… has not treated Naruto-kun well and we will not stand by and watch that. He will not be staying here anymore.”


 He just looked at the girl and spoke again. “I am sorry. It seems there has been some kind of misunderstanding here but we can talk and solve that.”


“No need.” The girl stepped towards the anbu and Danzo stepped between them.


“Oh and who are you to decide that? The boy is a property of this village and will be going nowhere. Anbu, capture them.” Danzo ordered.


“He is no one’s property.” Jiraiya shouted back.


“He is, as every jinchuriki, a strategic weapon of the village. Remember your place as a ninja of Konoha, Jiraiya. This is not time to let your emotions run wild.”


The girl stopped at the words and Jiraiya noticed the rage in the girl’s eyes at the statement, her fists clenched but she didn’t say anything.


He turned towards sensei and to his surprise the old man didn’t move but instead looked at the girl and spoke. “I am sorry but I cannot let you take Naruto away. He needs to stay here, in his home as his parents wanted. If you want to insist, I will have to use force to stop you.”


“You can try your best.” She replied and stepped forward.


That was the signal and one of the anbu attacked her with a drawn kunai. The girl didn’t stop and the kunai struck her directly in her forehead… only to shatter into pieces. The girl however instead of getting angry, smiled… a smile which sent shivers down Jiraiya’s spine. All his instincts screamed at him to run away or he would die.


The girl, no, the dragon spoke again. “The boss told me not to start any fights while I was here and I was only allowed to fight if I was attacked. This… counts as an attack, doesn’t it?”


And the next moment Jiraiya knew why his instincts were going haywire as an unholy amount of power seeped out from the girl.


Everyone in the room froze.


This power… it was like the Kyubi… no… It was probably worse…


Her figure blurred and all the anbu were thrown out of the hokage tower, their bodies ripping holes in the walls on their way out.


“Kanna, take Naruto-kun and go back. I will return in a few minutes.” She snapped a finger and a tear in space appeared near them.


The white haired girl nodded and took Naruto and jumped directly into the hole.


Danzo however attacked them with his Vacuum Sphere jutsu but the other girl who had been floating outside the window suddenly appeared between them and the attacks struck her… doing no damage.


“Catch them. Don’t let them escape!” Danzo shouted and everyone jumped into action.


The tear in space however closed and only two girls remained.


“Capture them! Don’t let them get away.” Sensei ordered and even Jiraiya joined them, even if he didn’t agree with their way of doing things.


“Are we having a fight, Tohru-chan? Hey, are we having a fight? Uwahahahaha these people want to fight us but they are so weak. Hey, do you think if we beat them up, Sil would be happy and then we can get extra desserts after dinner?”


“…well, the boss considers Naruto-kun his adopted brother so he would definitely not mind if we beat them up.” The girl answered.


“Good. Then for my extra dragon cherry pudding… get beaten up.”


Jiraiya had no idea what was happening but he had a feeling that things had just gone from bad to worse.

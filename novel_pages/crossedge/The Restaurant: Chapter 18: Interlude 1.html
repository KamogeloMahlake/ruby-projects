“Do we know where they came from? Where is this Summoning Land of Dragons? I have never heard of dragons as summons before in my life, even in the old scrolls of the Hyuga Clan.” Hiashi Hyuga asked.


“No one knew they existed before this. Not even the Toads of Mount Myoboku or Lady Katsuyu of Shikkotsu forest knew of them existing. We think they live in a completely different world separate from ours and Naruto was able to accidentally reverse summon himself there.”


“So… you are trying to say that all of this was just a big coincidence?” Shikaku Nara asked, sarcastically.


“No. The attack itself was due to the hasty decisions of Hiruzen Sarutobi and his advisors.” Fugaku Uchiha spoke for the first time, getting everyone’s attention.


“As Tsunade-sama stated just now… they had only attacked in retaliation. Moreover, their entire reason for getting angry was the unfair treatment of Naruto Uzumaki. What Tsunade-sama missed to mention was that they had called Naruto Uzumaki a possession and weapon of the village, further provoking the ire of the dragons and then attacked the green dragon girl first.” That was something Jiraiya hated too but he hadn’t said anything back then.


From an objective point of view, not losing the jinchuriki of the village was a priority task but Jiraiya knew that today people were not here to logically look at things but rather to find a scapegoat to blame everything on and sensei and his advisors… were just too convenient to pass up.


Sigh… he was right to stay away from all this politics.


Fugaku continued. “This matter would have never arisen had they done their work properly and treated Naruto Uzumaki as the Fourth wanted him to be treated. Even then, there was a chance that things wouldn’t have escalated to this degree if they hadn’t attacked first and tried to negotiate with a non-violent enemy first. That way, not only could we have the village’s jinchuriki back but also gained the support of such… powerful beings.”


“Is… is there no way to reconcile with them?” The Fire Daimyo asked meekly.


Tsunade shook his head. “We don’t know where they came from and where they went to. Naruto was… very lucky.” She then looked at Fugaku. “Do you have the reports?”


He nodded and put forward a scroll and unsealed it.


Several dozen files appeared on the table and he passed them over to every member on the table, including the Daimyo.


“Danzo Shimura, an advisor of the Hokage and the leader of a secret branch of anbu under his direct control called the root has been involved in too many things. The files in front of you contain the information about whatever we could find about him. Too bad he died and his body was destroyed or we could have the Yamanaka check through their memories to find the true extent of the damages he has caused.” Fugaku began as they all opened the files and began pursuing the contents.


The more he looked the deeper the frown on his face became.


Human Experiments, dealing with Orochimaru, kidnapping and assassinating members of Konoha Clans for techniques and kekkei genkai, colluding with nuke nin of other villages, looting villages and merchants to fund their missions, threatening orphanages to recruit. The root training regime and graduation test made him disgusted… was this the Konoha they preached about?


Was this the Will of Fire?


And Sensei was allowing all this to happen?


At this point, even Jiraiya couldn’t ignore the mistakes of his sensei… this was just too much.


The further he read, the more disgusted he became.


Sabotaging people, spreading false rumours and propaganda… Apparently it was them who had spread the information about Naruto being the reincarnation of the fox and sensei had tried to cover it up as him honouring Minato’s last wish…


That bastard was also involved in some shady deals during the nine tails attack and had been constantly spreading rumours about the Uchiha Clan in the village.


“In fact, these are only the things that we were able to find out from our investigation. With the help of a thorough search from the Yamanaka Clan, we can discover even more information from their minds.” Fugaku completed, remarkably keeping himself in control. Especially considering the fact that the death of Shisui Uchiha was caused by Danzo Shimura according to the reports.


“H-how can this be happening in Konoha?” The Fire Daimyo started. “Did Hiruzen know about this?”


“If he did then he was as guilty as Danzo Shimura for letting this happen and if he didn’t know anything was amiss, then he is clearly not competent enough to sit in his position anymore. As for what he is, you will have to ask him by yourself, Lord Daimyo.” Jiraiya felt bad but looked around and found that no one said anything to refute it.


“Either way, from what I have heard, he is not fit to continue as a Hokage after losing his arm and leg. The village has lost many ninjas, so soon after the Nine Tails attack. We have even lost the fourth, Orochimaru has rebelled and now this, not to mention the jinchuriki of the village has been taken by… Gods. We are in an incredibly dire situation right now with the other villages eying us. We need to elect a new Hokage as soon as possible and stabilise the situation.” Surprisingly, it was Hiashi Hyuga who said this.


Jiraiya knew he wasn’t wrong either but tried to remember and recalled the incident of the Kumo Head Ninja where Hiashi’s brother was sacrificed.


While he understood that it was the best possible choice, trading the life of one person for the like for hundreds in a potential war, especially right after the nine tails attack, he could also understand Hiashi’s anger with sensei about it, although the Hyuga Clan head was too good to show such a thing to anyone.


“Yes yes… then who is to become the next Hokage?” The Daimyo questioned.


“I propose Jiraiya-sama.”


“I think Tsunade-sama should take the seat. She is the granddaughter of the First and the grandniece of the Second, not to mention one of the Sannin. The village would regain its morale if she were to take the helm.”


Jiraiya raised his hand. “I do not wish to stand in the contest. My Spy Network needs me outside the village, more so now than before.”


“But that’s…”


At this time Tsunade also raised her hand. “I do not wish to take the position either but I recommend Fugaku Uchiha. The Uchiha Clan has been treated unfairly but even then has continued to contribute to the village. Fugaku and his son Itachi have been major contributors in the previous battle and he is more than capable of taking the position of the Hokage. In case everyone has forgotten, the Uchiha were one of the founders of the village too. Anyways, we can vote.”


Sure enough there were some doubts in the people while some didn’t agree with the choice but the vote cleared everything.


The only one who could stand in competition was Kakashi but he had been running around, weeding out spies.


Guy hadn’t been in the village during the attack. When he returned he was immediately sent out on another mission, not that anyone would recommend him as the Hokage.


Shikaku Nara directly pulled back.


In the end, only Hiashi Hyuga and Fugaku Uchiha were left.


And so… Fugaku Uchiha became the fifth Hokage of Konohagakure.

- Hiashi Hyuga -


“How could that happen? Fugaku Uchiha, the new Hokage?” The elders clamoured between one another.


Their pride had been hurt when their biggest rival, the Uchiha had become a hokage.


Normally, this would have been impossible.


Every Clan Head knew how the Uchiha were treated by the upper management of the village, not to mention how the selection of the hokage happened.


It was always someone from the Hokage line.


From First Hokage to the Second, then Third and even the fourth was the student of the Third’s student.


Even last time, the only recommendations for the position of the Hokage were Orochimaru and Minato, both people related to the Third Hokage…


If some gained a strength and reputation comparable to them but wasn’t from the same line… they were removed.


A prime example would be Sakumo Hatake… or how could the information about such an important mission become public knowledge?


Everyone knew what was happening but no one interfered, afraid of facing the same end as the White Fang.


So, an Uchiha becoming the Hokage was the last thing anyone could imagine, especially after the Nine Tails fiasco… but who could have thought?


In the end, Hiashi’s name was given just so there was some competition. With Tsunade and Jiraiya backing away, and Tsunade recommending Fugaku, his ascension was all but decided. The Fire Daimyo was also in a hurry and quickly agreed to this.


But what surprised Hiashi was what happened later.


Fugaku Uchiha’s first decision as the Hokage was to strip the elder council of their position and have them searched thoroughly by the Yamanaka.


If they found anything awry, the search would then move to Hiruzen Sarutobi too. Everyone knew they would find something, after all, they had been at the top for several decades. It would be a miracle if they hadn’t done shady things, especially with Danzo and Orochimaru running around.


The only relief for Sarutobi was that the investigation’s results will not be made public so as to not cause too much chaos in the village during this time.


Now if that first decision was expected, the next one was completely out of the blue.


Fugaku Uchiha selected his new council and while many if not most expected him to choose from his own clan, he actually chose Tsunade, Shikaku and… him…


Yes… the Uchiha Clan head had chosen his clan’s biggest rival’s head as an advisor.


Third was the decision of dividing the duties.


Uchiha had always managed the Police Force alone. This had always put them at odds with the Konoha civilians. To reduce this, he had opened the police force to other clans.


The Hyuga were chosen for this as their Byukagan could help monitor the village just as well as the Sharingan could.


With just this small move, Fugaku showed people that he was not favouring his clan and actually taking power away from them whereas what it did was share their burden with other clans, removing the Uchiha as the sole target of the public’s ire when law was enforced.


As for taking away the power, Hiashi was sure the Anbu’s composition would change soon enough so no hair was lost on their heads.


It even made Hiashi doubt if the position of the Hokage suddenly made everyone a better politician because these were not things he could have expected from the previous Fugaku.


Perhaps the… Gods did knock some sense into him while beating him up?


Yes, Gods…


It was something Tsunade had heard herself and after the show of power… no one doubted it either.


The purpose of the Fire Daimyo coming here… was to make Shrines to appease the wrath of the Gods…


Apparently, one of the people investigating the incident from his court was also a member of the Fire Temple.


Safe to say the monk was beyond appalled at the news that actual Gods had descended and punished Konoha…


They were currently having the shrines constructed in place of the Hokage Mountain as a show of their apology.


The Fire Daimyo himself wanted to inaugurate the shrine in a festival while the monks from the Fire Temple had been called to offer prayers there.


Hiashi and the Hyuga Clan were in charge of this entire operation and he had sent his assistant, Haru Hyuga with his daughter Hinata to check the location before the construction could begin.


As the meeting finished, he stepped out of the room and saw his daughter who had returned, carrying… a bunny.


Sigh… Hinata… he was always worried about what she might face if she continued to be so kind.


Perhaps, his new position could allow him to somehow change the age-old traditions of his clan.


After all, if an Uchiha could become the Hokage… there was no way the Hyuga couldn’t change.

Ikki seemed to be having fun training there and there was still time before his school reopened so he decided to stay there for training. He said he will go to the Ryozanpaku masters for training after this.


It was getting late, so we left and went back to our restaurant. But before leaving, I left a card of my restaurant with the girl whose house I had almost destroyed.


I felt kinda bad for her.


The stay in Kisuke's world was full of ups and downs but at least the candies in his store were something.


That reminds me, I need other helpers in the kitchen.


Maybe I can ask Nakiri-san since he was the Principal of a Culinary School or something? He could introduce me to some of his students who can help me out. In fact, I needed some new waitresses for my Restaurant too.


With most of the girls being busy with teaching the Oni and setting up the business. That only left me with Tohru as a waitress.


“Boss, I heard we are having a party?” My musings were broken by Tohru’s words. She looked excited.


“Yes. A week later, we are having a party.” I had earned a lot in the deal with Issac-san and could easily afford to splurge here. “In fact, you can invite your friends if you want.”


“Really!”


“Yup. Kanna is often with Naruto and the Dojo Masters so she must be missing the other dragon friends too. It will be a good chance for them to reunite and have some fun.” I suggested.


“Alright, I will bring some of my friends too.”


“Just make sure they don’t cause any trouble.”


“Trust me, Boss.”


Rimuru had also gone out to invite Shizuka-san for the party and I had asked Cid-kun’s friends to invite others too.


It would be a good opportunity for all the guests to gather and also give me time to prepare everything in advance.


Moreover, they could bring their friends and that could increase my customer base.


As I was thinking of this, the door to the Restaurant opened and some familiar figures popped in.


Kisuke-san had come with the orange haired girl, Orihime, the black haired girl, Rukia, the carrot top Ichigo and… Yoruichi.


“Is this the place? A maid cafe?” Ichigo said, looking around the Restaurant.


“It’s not a maid cafe, just a simple Restaurant.” I replied. “Tohru, take their orders.”


“Yes.”


As Tohru took their orders, I talked with Kisuke-san.


The orange haired girl called Orihime was looking around the area nervously.


"Umm, hello." She said, attracting our attention.


"Hello. Welcome to my restaurant. What will you be having today?" I asked politely.


"Ano… I… you are the one who saved me, aren't you?"


"... I am. Are you alright now?" I asked. She had been unconscious when we left so we never got a chance to go back.


"Ah yes. Thank you for helping me… and my brother." She bowed deeply.


"It's fine. The… supernatural police of that world would have done that anyway." I said turning towards Rukia and Ichigo.


"Ohh…" She replied awkwardly, not knowing what to say.


"So, what will you be having today?"


"This…" She hesitated.


Kisuke-san spoke on her behalf.  "She will be having a job at your restaurant if you have one."


"Say what?"


Kisuke went on to explain the situation.


She was living alone after her brother's death. Her parents had died even before her brother and after her brother's death, she was given an allowance by her aunt who stayed far away.


The incident that day had caused her house to crash. The landlord was an ass and had evicted her from the place and made her pay for the damage. Partly because there were no culprits found for the incident. Now, that part was taken care of by her aunt but she had reduced her allowance by a lot because she too thought that the girl was making up stories.


… talk about a messed up situation.


"So you are looking for a part time job in my Restaurant?"


She bowed. "Please."


"Hmm, well alright. The damage was partially caused by me because I interfered in the work of whatever soul police was doing back then. I think I can take responsibility for this."


I explained to her the schedule. I did need some more staff in the restaurant. It's not like I didn't have any empty rooms. We had expanded both the house and the Restaurant just a week ago.


“Hey, Hat-and-clogs, are you sure this is a good place to work? It totally looks like a shady maid cafe and not a good one at that.” The carrot top spoke, making my eyes twitch.


“Excuse me, what do you mean by ‘not so good maid cafe’?” I asked, my tone calm, as I reigned in my anger.


Who the fuck did this brat think he was, dissing my Restaurant?


Even Dragons and Demon Lords had to politely sit in line to be served here.


“What else? Your Restaurant is obviously empty and even the one waitress is a cosplayer pretending to be who knows what?”


“I am a dragon, little boy.” Tohru replied, hotly and then looked at me. “Do you want me to bash his face, boss?” Tohru asked, not hiding her anger


“Now now, Tohru, what have we learned about customer service?” I assuaged her anger and looked at the carrot top. “Tell me, have you ever seen a Restaurant which is always full?” I asked.


He opened his mouth to reply but shut up again.


“As for my waitress, as she has already said, she is a dragon. A genuine Dragon. Do you perhaps have a problem with them?”


To his credit he didn’t say anything and apologised too.


Rukia on the other hand looked at Kisuke, “But Dragons are…”


“Tohru-chan is not that kind of Dragon. So don’t worry about it” He reassured her and then explained a bit about this place.


They ordered. Yoruichi, ordering milk and some fish.


I went in and cooked their orders while Tohru happily showed Orihime around the place and also the work clothes they were supposed to wear.


“By the way, what is Ikki doing?” Tohru asked.


“Oh, he was too tired and is sleeping. We will be having a packed meal for him too.” Kisuke-san ordered.


“Sure. I will get it arranged. Oh, by the way, there is a Party in the Restaurant, a week from now. You are invited and can bring friends too.”


“Oh my, that sounds like fun. Did something good happen for you to be throwing a party?” He asked.


“Ah yes. I signed a big deal with Issac-san and we are also opening a new Publishing House. The Restaurant also had a renovation. Finally, I had a bit of time on my hand and decided to throw a party to share my happiness with everyone.”


“Congratulations! I will be happy to join.”


I sat down and looked at them as they ate.


Even Yoruichi was eating with full concentration.


“One more.” Rukia ordered, her mouth still puffed with food.


“Sure.”


“One more of me too.” Yoruichi’s voice sounded before changing her order. “Actually, make it two more.”


“In a minute.”


“Damn Kisuke, keeping this place all to yourself. Why didn’t you tell me about this place before?”


“Ahahaha… well, it’s quite expensive eating out everyday and…”


“So you were just being a cheapskate. That's it. Silas, pack fifteen, no twenty of these fishes for me. Kisuke will pay the bill.”


“Yoruichi…”


The banter went on for some time and they left after they were done.


Orihime will be shifting here by tonight.


She will be working as a waitress here during the evenings as she had school in the mornings. I agreed as I needed more help in the evenings.


- Naruto -


“And Ninjas can also make bunshin but I can’t do that, ya know. It’s always that crappy bunshin jutsu.”

“Ah, not really. I wasn’t able to learn any before coming here, but other kids might know. They had clans and clan people had many jutsu of their own.” He was a bit miffed. By now, he had figured out that he was the son of the fourth Hokage. Although he didn’t understand why his own father would seal the fox inside him, he didn’t want to blame him without knowing his side of the story.

Miu nee-chan and Shigure nee-chan were busy and they had a holiday in the academy today. He had even finished his training for today.

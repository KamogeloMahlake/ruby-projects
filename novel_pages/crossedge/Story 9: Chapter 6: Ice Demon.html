“Exactly.” I turned towards the Prime Minister who had an extremely ugly look on his face. “I can access an almost infinite number of worlds, some stronger, some weaker, some with no life while some where literal Gods live….so tell me, why would I be interested in taking over your little Empire? It’s just….foolish.”


Not giving them any other chance to speak, I continued. “With the knowledge in my possession, I can make an Empire far more advanced than this with absolute ease. But I am not interested in ruling. The things I want, I can get by the current setup without harming the Empire. So….if you want, you can worry and spend your nights without any sleep or….you can just put aside your fears for a while and see my work first. Any doubts?”


The guy kept quiet.


Good.


I turned towards the people in the room again. “If you choose to work for me, you will become strong, learn things which will make you able to take down even Super Class Danger Beasts alone and bring the Empire to much greater heights but….you will have similar restrictions as well. You will not be able to participate in any political or private job or use the information you learn here for private profit. You will have to follow my orders without questions....no matter what. If I tell you to kill, you kill, if I tell you to die, you die. I will permit no questions. Understood”


The group nodded.


“That said, I am not a heartless monster….I do not have the habit of harming my subordinates without any reasons. But if you ever….betray the Empire or me….the type of death Honest got….would feel like a blessing to you.” I stared at the group. “Anyone who doesn’t want to stay….step aside.”


Slowly, around six people stepped aside.


I waited for a minute more but the rest didn’t move.


“Alright.” I brought out my wand and their eyes widened while some of them faltered but I wasn’t going to kill anyone. “Obliviate.”


The six of them paused.


I looked at the Emperor and nodded.


“General Budo, see them out. Please assure that they get proper rest and don’t talk to them about today’s matters.”


The man looked at the Emperor and then at me for a second longer than I would have liked and then bowed to the Emperor as he led the obliviated group outside.


It was then that the silver haired girl in the group which stayed back spoke. “What did you do?” Her eyes widened as she realised that she had said those words out loud. “I apologis….”


“I erased their memories of this meeting. They will remember nothing of today.” I replied, making the eyes of several people widened.


“Your first task is to establish a base according to the requirements I have stated on the land given by the Emperor. As for your joining gifts.” I took out the swords I had bought from Wiz and presented to them. “From today on, you are the members of my team, the Ace in the Hole.”

A day has passed since the creation of ‘Ace in the Hole’.


Managing things on the other side was hard, especially when I wasn’t there all the time to monitor things.


The Prime Minister didn’t say it out loud but he didn’t trust me….and neither did most of the people who stayed back.


In fact, several of them wanted to act as spies for the Empire despite the words of the Emperor.


That sly Prime Minister had sent his own daughter, Spear as a spy to monitor the work of my team, all in the guise of showing a sign of cooperation towards me. 


I need to convince and make them loyal to me as well as teach them a few things. Fortunately, I had the methods for that.


I let out a sigh.


So many things to do….


I needed someone to help me out with everything.


I trusted Lavinia but she was a bit naive and….too young. I didn’t want to drag her into this for a while. I need others too.


There was also the upcoming Ultimate Class Magician Examination in a few days.


That said, there was no easy way to become strong.


All the work which I put in the Empire right now will yield far greater results later on.


That world is almost unexplored with great potential. Their civilisation is not as advanced as many others and is a place where I can easily establish a solid footing.


This much work….is reasonable.


I shook my head and got up. I didn’t have time to slack off.


At this time I sensed another summoning.


The summonings were getting more frequent now.


I opened the portal and on the other side was a new face.


It was a slender boy with sleek white-blond hair, cold grey eyes, a pale complexion and rather sharp, pointed features.


The surprising thing was the clothes he was wearing.


I was quite familiar with those clothes.


Hogwarts Uniform.


The boy looked stunned at the appearance of the portal and stumbled and fell on his back.


“I….it works. It actually works!” he exclaimed.


“Yes yes, it works. Get over it. I am a very busy man. Who are you and what is it that you desire?” I asked, casually.


“You….what are you?” The boy questioned, pointing his finger at me.


I smiled, putting on a show. “I….am a Devil. Now, on with it? Why have you summoned me?”


The boy was shit scared when he heard what I was but slowly, a look of smug arrogance came on his face as he pointed his finger at me.


“Shut up. I have summoned you and you will serve me. Stop ordering me around.” The boy snarned.


….


….


“About that….I am not that type of devil. If you want something from me, you will have to make a deal. Strength, knowledge, tools, everything for a reasonable price. That said….” I let out a bit of my Demonic Power on him, making him crumble on his knees. “I don’t like being ordered around by no name little twerps like you. Now….who are you?”


The boy shook in fear. “I-I am Draco Malfoy.”


….


Oh….


That makes so much sense.


“Hoh. So tell me Draco Malfoy, what is it that you desire?”


“Power….make me the strongest wizard alive.”


“The strongest wizard alive? You mean stronger than Dumbledore and Tom Riddle?”


“Tom Riddle?”


“The guy who calls himself Lord Voldemort. It’s actually an anagram of his real name Tom Marvolo Riddle.”


His face paled at the information.


“Anyways, I can easily do that.”


I actually could. Just give him a spoonful of the Demon’s extract and boom.


But I had something better than that in mind.


“I can give you a method of becoming strong enough to knock out Dumbledore in a single attack. But….what will you pay? Oh and before you say the same old cliche things, I don’t trade in souls. Money, knowledge and tools….those are the only things I am interested in. So….go and tell your father to summon me. And I will make the deal.” I snapped my fingers and several ice butterflies were created around him and flew out in the dorms.


Those were actually summoning contracts transfigured into butterflies.


The Slytherins were the richest of the Wizarding world and had the most resources too, the perfect kind of people to make deals with.


Let’s see who summons me next….


The guy in front of me tried to speak but quickly held his tongue with a single glare from my side.


“Oh….and this consultation will cost you five hundred galleons. I expect the payment in a week or your entire family gets a Malediction Curse. Bye.” I closed the portal without giving him any chance to speak. The five hundred galleons will make sure that he consults his father.


The Malfoy House is loaded….


I wonder just how much I can swindle out of them?


As I was making plans, I sensed another summoning.


Wow.


Two in a row.


I quickly collected myself and opened the portal and got ready to do my usual presentation.


What I didn't expect was someone diving directly into my face through the hole and falling on me.

There were a lot of noises in the background from the other side of the portal.


But I wasn’t concentrating on that.


I felt something soft on my lips.


The room was lit by a flash of light as the figure wrapped its arms around my neck and I realised that the figure was kissing me.


I held back my instinct to freeze everything in my surroundings.


Lavinia and the Himejima family were still in the building.


I quickly gained my composure and pushed the figure.


Taking a clear look, she was a girl with burnt orange hair with long side plaits and orange eyes. She was wearing a loose-fitting white dress which resembled a cheongsam and glasses.


However the most eye-catching thing about her were the golden lights which almost seemed like wings behind her.


For a moment, I had thought she was an Angel.


I just stared at her in awe for a moment before my attention was diverted by the noise behind her.


“She escaped there.”


“Is it another one of her accomplice? A space type ability?”


There were several girls and behind them, men in black suits who were pointing at the portal.


“Move aside.” A greyish haired, black clothed girl commanded as the others moved aside and she looked at us inside the portal. “How interesting.” She licked her lips in a way which gave me very mixed feelings.


And then without any warning, she drew a sword and dashed towards the portal.


“How annoying?” I stopped the thrust sword with a reinforced Ice Sword, making her eyes widen. She wasn’t weak….but she wasn’t that strong either.


I put more power behind my attack and sent her flying into a wall nearby.


“Captain!”

 

“Impossible?!”


They faced me with their weapons drawn.


I sighed and put aside my sword, drawing my wand as I sent several of my summoning contracts through the portal.


The girl from before got up quickly and shot towards me once more.


I was however done playing so I directly froze the entire group up to their necks.


Looking directly into the eyes of the grey haired woman, I spoke.


“Don’t bother. You’re weak.”


Her pupils shrank and then her face morphed into a crazy look as she burst out into a maniacal laughter.


I just closed the portal in front of her.


As for my questions, the girl here could answer me.


I looked at her to see her kneeling before me.


She looked at me with eyes full of adoration and spoke. “What are your orders, my Ashikabi?”

“Huh?!”

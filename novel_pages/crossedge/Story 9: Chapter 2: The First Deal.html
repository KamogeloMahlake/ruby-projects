So, when he went back home that day, I followed him and hid in his home.


When he went to sleep, I knocked him out and tied him up, throwing him in my trunk.


The guy was at the Mid Class level but we had stuff which can bind even Mid Class Demonic Beasts and restrict their Demonic Energy. I snatched some of his hair first.


When he was completely incapacitated, I woke him up.


The man was groggy from the sudden injury but he did recognise me when he woke up.


“You! So it was you.”


I smacked him on his face a few times with a mallet, not hard enough to kill him but certainly enough to break his face.


“Only I ask questions here… and you answer… truthfully. If this rule is broken anytime, I will kill you. Follow this rule and I will let you meet your brother.” I brought out the emblem of the Demonic Hand that his brother used to carry. The symbol of a group leader.


“You don’t know who you are messing with. The people behind me… aghhhhh.”


I had already stabbed his thigh to shut him up.


Next I brought a vial of poison which could be used on weapons to kill Demonic Beasts.


“I have neither the time nor patience for your foolishness, so answer me. Why did you send your brother after me?”


“I didn’t…”


I poured the poison in the stab wound on his thigh.


The man paled immediately.


“I repeat, I’m in a hurry. And now you are the same. So, shall we get this game up to the pace? Answers.”


“I -I will answer. The antidote.”


“Will only be given when I am done with my questioning. No negotiations. Why did you send your brother after me?”


“W-we do this sometimes. The o-wner of the shop sends people to hunt for devils without any kind of backing and get back his magic books and artifacts so that he can sell them again. We divide the money.”


….

Everyone of them… every single one of them is the same.


“Does the Marbas house know of this?”


“N-no. They don’t and wouldn’t care even if they did. We only target weaker Devils.”


….


Son of a bitch.


“Who else knows of this?”


“Some people of the Demonic Hand and the owner. We don’t let the news spread or the Marbas house’s reputation will sink and they will kill us.”


I asked him a few more questions and then made him drink the antidote. I still needed him for something, but just to make sure, I even used some sedatives used against Demonic Beasts to keep him out. 


With my targets clear, I made some quick changes to the plan.


Time was of the essence so I acted quickly.


I used his hair in the polyjuice potion and drank it. With the amount I had, I could keep up the disguise for a few hours.


Just enough for my plan.


I headed straight to the Magic Shop.


The owner was a Mid Class Devil working for the Marbas House.


“Grant? Why are you here so late?”


“I forgot something here.” I replied and went behind the counter.


He didn’t pay any attention to me so I took this opportunity to fling one of the sedative bombs at his face.


He was not expecting this and was taken aback. I used this opportunity to stab him through the eye using a reinforced poisoned dagger I had picked up from my first attackers.


The man was able to put his hand in front of his eyes but I had used this same move to cleave through the Mid Class Demonic Beast’s heads, skulls and all.


His hand was pierced and then the dagger went through his eye, killing the man directly.


With the man down, I quickly got to work.


I hurried out and brought an enchanted trunk I had kept there. I could have kept it with me but then I would have needed to enlarge it and there were cameras in the shop.


I had already covered the trunks with a different kind of cover which didn’t bore any resemblance with my own trunks.


Over the next ten minutes, I threw everything I could get my hands on from the shop and threw them into the trunk and ran out of the shop with the trunk.


In fact there were so many Magic Circles in front of me but I didn’t touch any of them in fear of getting tracked down.


There is going to be a catastrophe coming to this place soon enough and I didn’t want them to get any whiff of me.


I went directly to his home which had already been arranged to look as if he had packed things in a hurry and ran away.


Just to make things more credible, I threw one small bell type artifact in the corner of the room, under the table.


If they searched properly, they would find the artifact.


After that I went straight to the pits and buried the trunk deep into a hole near a place I had marked previously.


With everything done, I threw away all the clothes I had been wearing and burned them.


I had left Grant in the Pits as well and waited till he was attacked and killed by a Mid Class Demonic Beast.


Using this bait, I even hunted the beast.


The effect of the Polyjuice had worn off by then so I bought a Demonic Beast corpse and used its claws to make injuries on myself and left the pit.


Sure enough, by the time I was back, there was chaos in the whole town.

Two months had passed since that incident.


The Marbas House had sent a High Class Devil with a dozen Mid Class Devils immediately to respond to the entire situation.


They had investigated and their search had led them to Grant and by an extension, his brother’s Demonic Hand.


Them going missing a while ago had actually worked against them in fact as they were all interrogated and they disappeared as well.


I was left completely alone in all this.


Still, it was horrifying to see a few small Hunting Groups get destroyed so easily.


No one even questioned anything in this territory.


Not only them, many others had died and mysteriously disappeared.


Were the lives of Low Class Devils so cheap?


I once again solidified my will to rise above these people.


I will not be helpless.


A few things have changed in the town since then.


Shops in the town had increased security while the prices had increased.


There were posters of Grant and the ones I had killed all over the town as well as in other territories with a reward on their heads.


Other Hunting groups were acting quiet too.


I had taken some time to heal up and recover in the meanwhile and practised Occlumency and my Clan Trait in my spare time and read the books on Magic and Rituals which I had gotten from the Magic Shop, the reason why those bastards came after me in the first place. They contained some methods of making one stronger.


Mostly sacrifices which were all banned now. There were a few methods but they were risky and vague.


Basically, this book was a massive waste.


Another book contained the list of identified Demonic Beasts and their uses as well as information about the Pits.


This one was far more useful.


The last book was on Charm and Hypnosis Magic for Devils which I found to be the most useful.


Apart from this, I was able to buy something to pass time and remember my last life.


A video game.


Yes.


Since the rise in the number of Reincarnated Devils, the underworld was slowly accommodating their technologies.


Televisions and to some extent games are becoming common nowadays.


Dave had taken the wand to the Runesmith and had made a frame for it using a magical metal which conducted Demonic Power. This added to the overall durability of the wand.


Although I will have to pay off a debt for the metal for a while.


I had completed the plan where I could sell my gold off in a covert way. The only reason I was avoiding doing so was due to the Marbas incident.


But I couldn’t keep on avoiding this for long.


It was during such a time that I finally got another summoning.


Closing all the doors and windows of my house, I first wore the Ravenclaw’s Diadem and covered it under a hat and then responded to the summoning.


The one on the other end was a kid.


He had green, shoulder-length hair which was braided on the sides and green eyes. He wore clothes which wouldn’t be off on a noble kid.


Why do I always have kids summoning me?


“W-who are you?” he asked hesitantly. “We command you to answer us.”


….


Command.


This brat?


But then again, I had no idea what kind of world this was so let’s find out.


“My name is Ace Belphegor. Who am I speaking to?” I asked politely, passively using my Charm Magic to ease the kid up to questioning.


“W-we are the Emperor of the Empire.”


….what?


That didn’t tell me anything.


I still helped back and enquired more.


“Oh, that sounds amazing. An Emperor at such a young age. You must be very talented.” I praised and the kid puffed up his chest in pride. “Pardon my ignorance. I’m not from your world so… would you tell me something more about your empire?”


“You are not from our world!” he asked, surprised.


“Yes. I am from a different world.” I brought out my wings for the kid to see.


“How amazing? Is that a Teigu?”


A Teigu?


That word brought some memories to the front of my mind and I looked at the kid in front of me.


Teigu.


Emperor.


It couldn’t be.


Just to make sure I talked to him for a while and found out that he had just ascended to the throne.


The kid looked like he was seven or eight.


It was easy to get some things about the current world out of him.


The Empire hadn’t gone completely into hell yet.


I thought of what to do while I was talking to him.


For a good future contractor, it would be for the best if the Empire became stronger.


The kid himself wanted to do good for the country and it was only his naivety which fucked him over.


I had always felt bad for the kid whom no one actually tried to help.


Reminded me of my own situation.


But more than that, having the Emperor of a strong and united Empire as my contractor will be far more useful than anyone else I could think of.


So I conjured up a plan.


He was quite trusting of Honest.


I didn’t try to break his trust right now or it could push him away from me. What I needed first was to make him trust me.


“Well to be honest Your Majesty, the paper you got was a contract which can be used to summon me. In my world, we need to make contracts to get promoted. For this, you can tell us your desire and we would try to fulfil it if it is possible for us for an appropriate cost though.”


“Our desires?”


“Yes. For example if you want something to eat which you haven’t ever tasted before or something new which you haven’t seen, I can provide you with it for a suitable price.”


“Ohhh Really? Can you show me something?”


“Why not? Here, have a try for free.”


I bought some chocolates from my bag and passed it to him.


The kid ate those without any kind of hesitation at all and his eyes lit up.


“Amazing! This is the best sweet we have ever eaten. Give us some more.” he shouted.


“As you wish, Your Majesty, but first, we need to discuss the terms of the deal.”


“Discuss?”


“Yes. I am very new to this so I have to follow some rules or I will be punished.”


“Oh. Very well. You have gifted us with the most delicious sweets we have ever eaten, we cannot let you be punished or it will tarnish the name of the Empire. State your rules.”


Oh he was such a nice kid.


The more I talked to him, the more bad I felt about his ending.


“First of all, you cannot tell anyone about our deals. Not even your closest friends or subordinates.”


“Very well.”


“Second, you can only summon me when you are alone.”


“Hmm hmm.”


“Third, we will discuss the price of every deal before the exchange. As time goes on, I can trade better things with you but the prices will vary accordingly.”


“Okay, we understand.”


“In that case, I look forward to our prosperous future together, Your Majesty.”


“Hahahaha we will look forward to it too.”

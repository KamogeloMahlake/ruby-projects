- Arthas -


I looked around the place.


The Sabaody Archipelago… It looked exactly like what I remembered from the anime. It was actually a massive mangrove forest growing out from the middle of the ocean with each tree of the forest serving as an island on which people live.


“Beautiful, isn’t it?” The captain asked as he got down near us.


“The whole island is actually a mangrove forest. It’s made up of seventy nine Yarukiman Mangrove trees. The Yarukiman Mangrove trees are the largest mangroves in the world and are strong enough to grow from the bottom of the ocean to the top.Each tree is called a grove and serves as an island for which people live on. People can get from one grove to another via the various bridges built connecting around the many tree roots.”


“It’s indeed beautiful. I would love to visit a place like this during the holidays.” Veena said and it was actually that beautiful. I would agree with her too had I not known about what kind of place this island was.


Thankfully, the captain informed everyone in my stead. “I’d hate to disappoint you Miss but this place is not suitable for spending holidays, especially areas one to twenty nine. Always stay in a group when you are there. The best way to look around while being protected is to get the protection of some gang in these islands. You know what? Here, take this. It will help you out.” The man gave us a map of the island describing where everything was.


Richard had told us about the places we had to visit but the map had more details. I took a brief look at the man and checked the place we had to visit.


The Auction House was in Grove number one.


We need to register there first before we can go to the hotel area which was from grove number seventy to seventy nine.


I also needed to search for Shakky’s Rip Off Bar and see if I can get any achievements here.


Grove number forty to forty nine was a tourist area and the place where most of the shops were. We needed to check those places too. There could be some good things for sale there. So much to do in such a short time.


The captain went with his crew and we left our own way.


“Let’s meet Richard’s contact in the Hotel Area and get a place to stay first. We can plan our visit there. The captain did tell us not to move alone. It’s better to stick around for now.”


“Alright.”


And that’s what we did. We were in Grove number fifty three. Grove number fifty to fifty nine was the shipping area and sixty to sixty nine was the residential area for the locals.


We moved towards the Hotel Area first. Each grove was separate and was connected by bridges built around the ginormous tree roots.


“Bubble service, bubble service!” A man was shouting, making us look at him.


We saw a person go to him with several large bags and pay him some money.  The man then pulled out what looked like a horn, dipped it in a bucket and blew out a huge bubble around the bags. To the surprise of my group, the bubble floated, carrying the goods in the air. He quickly tied a rope around the goods and handed it to the person who carried everything like a balloon.


I had hazy memories of seeing this in the anime in my past life but man if that wasn’t weird… and amazing to see it in front of my eyes.


Similarly, we saw another person riding on what looked like a jet ski, only this one had a bubble under it and floated slightly above the ground.


On asking around, we found out that the island offered the Bubble Taxi, the name of those jet ski, for rent to the tourists.


Since they needed the bubbles to function which can only be made by the fresh resin of these mangroves, they couldn’t be used outside and there was no use in stealing them. Moreover, there was no doubt that they didn’t have some kind of backup.


Remembering what little I could about this place, I wouldn’t doubt if there was a bomb inside those bikes which would be set off should they be taken away from the archipelago. The more I thought about it, the more likely it seemed too.


I shook my head. No need to worry about it.


We were not trying to steal anything. We decided to travel by foot to enjoy the view.


It took us around an hour to reach the hotel area. Along the way, I checked several people. T5 were quite common in this place. There were several T6 too.


Then again, it was understandable or else there was no way an island like this would have remained independent despite being in the middle of so many kingdoms and with so many pirates roaming around the area. The island must have the backing of some very strong people, probably not a T8 but at least a group of T7 for it to remain standing till now.


We reached the place mentioned by Richard.


Lion’s Brew, a pub with a hotel attached to it. The owner was someone from our kingdom and did business with Richard.


We entered and asked for Richard’s associate at the reception table.


“Hello, I am Baron Arthas from the Utrudragon Empire. I was sent here by Richard Clyde of the Dolphin’s Song Trading Agency looking for one Damon Artwick. I handed him the recommendation letter from Richard.” I showed him my baron badge and the man nodded.


“Please wait in the reception for a while. I will send a message to the boss.”


“Thank you.”


We waited for fifteen minutes and were escorted inside by an attendant.


Damon Artwick was a fierce looking man.


He was built like a tank, had short blonde hair but his most prominent feature was probably the scar running from his right left cheek to his left jaw.


A simple scan at him showed his Class to be a Mafia Boss, a T6 Class.


Damn…

“Five.”

“Here you go. My attendant will take you to the Auction House on the day of the auction. You will be staying here, correct?”

“As the name says, they are said to be fruits with devils inside them. Each of them is cursed by the Sea Devil. Anyone who consumes one gains supernatural powers but becomes hated by the sea. “What can they do?”


“The Zoan type allows the user to transform into animals or birds or even other races, increasing their power and giving them new powers. The Logia type is the rarest and most expensive one. These  fruits that grant the power to create, control, and transform one's body into an element of nature. When transforming into their element, a Logia user becomes physically invulnerable, or outright intangible in the case of certain elements, and retains control over their body even if separated into pieces. But these are just the start. Their power increases with the power of the users.”

“Oh sure it is. It’s just that… the power they offer… is far beyond your imagination. Logia users can permanently change the terrain or even the climate of an island. Tell me, why would people care about such minor disadvantages if that were the case?”

Her lessons with Euclid had taught her various ways to use her Sacred Gear and after her fight with the Demonic Beasts when she had attained her Balance Breaker for the first time, she had experimented more with her Sacred Gear too.


Finally, she was able to develop a Subspecies Balance breaker for her Absolute Demise similar to Tobi-kun’s Balance Breaker with his Canis Lykaon.


Demi-chan merged with her body, becoming a blue and white coloured armour, a tiara of ice appeared on her head, keeping her hair in place, preventing them from interfering with her fight and lastly wings of ice covered her devil wings, giving them a feeling of an angel’s wings only made of ice.


This form of her Sacred Gear not only increased her magic power and Ice Magic but also boosted her physical stats, something she has had plenty of time to train. Being a member of Mathias Buné Zagan’s peerage essentially meant she had to train her physique. So although not at the level of Mathias and the rest, she was confident about taking down High Class beings with her physique alone.


Her preparations were completed just in time as she felt another aura spike nearby. Looking in the direction, she felt the heat wave emanating from the eight headed fire serpent made of Holy Fire… eight headed hydra.


It felt almost draconic and there was a hint of something else in the Holy Flames that made the attack feel more dangerous to her.


The flame serpent roared, destroying the ice wall and setting many exorcists and magicians in its path on fire.


She waved her hand, creating a barrier of ice and meeting the attack head on.


The ice and fire met together in an intense explosion, covering the whole area with vapours, blocking everyone’s vision. She flew out of the cloud of vapours and saw several heads of the hydra coming towards her and attacked them with giant spears of ice, cutting them off.


However, suddenly something surprising happened.


From the heads which were cut off, two more heads rose up and came racing towards her, making her quickly take to the skies to dodge the attacks, sending waves of icicles towards the heads of the hydra to cover her retreat.


Once more, as the heads were destroyed, two more took their place.


“Fascinating isn’t it? Just a bit of research and good enough materials could boost the abilities of Sacred Gears to such an extent, even a Longinus.” She heard the voice of the woman she had hated for years for killing her former teacher. The woman came out from the cloud of vapour.


It had taken her witnessing Glenda alive and willingly working with the same woman she had hated.


The truth had broken her but it had also helped her let go of her hate for Augusta. Sure enough when she saw her today, she couldn’t feel any hate for the woman… at least for the past.


“You seem to have developed quite nicely yourself. I didn’t know the Absolute Demise had a Balance Breaker like this. Was it because of you becoming a Devil? Are there any reactions between the Sacred Gears and Evil Pieces? Or is it because of the special food items created by the Zagan boy? I can’t wait to find it out for myself.”


Lavinia felt an intense burst of anger when she heard her mention Mathias and her plans for him. “If you…”


“Hmm? Are you saying something?”


“If you even harm a single hair on Mathias' head, I will end you.” She warned.


“Oh my, such big words. Have you forgotten what I did to your teacher?” The woman mocked.


“You did nothing. I already know everything, the whole truth about the Witch of the South, Glenda Goodwitch and I have nothing to do with such a person anymore.”


She recognised the look of surprise on the woman’s face.“Ah, so you know already. Good. I don’t have to keep this act up anymore. To be honest, I always thought Glenda was too cautious, fearing the Longinus would go berserk if you threw a tantrum and going through all this drama. There was always the option of finishing you off  but I am thankful she didn’t do that or we wouldn’t have such great research materials in our hands. Still, it looks like I will have to put some effort into it. “


She took a look towards the area where Matty was fighting. “It looks like Satanael is having problems with your King and might need my help. Do you think his body would be damaged too much if I attacked him seriously with the Holy Flame? We don’t want to destroy such precious material after all.”


That was the last straw for her as she sent waves of icicles towards the woman.


“Haven’t you learned, girl? It’s useless to use such tricks on me.” Augusta replied as the Incinerate Anthem tanked the attacks, evaporating the ice and creating vapours between them again.


Lavinia however didn’t reply and instead created body doubles of Ice and spread them in the cloud of vapours.


The ploy worked as the serpents began to attack the ice doubles. The doubles were created and controlled by using Demi-chan’s powers and had semi-sentience, enabling them to move and attack by themselves. It wasn’t enough to stop Augusta but it was more than enough to buy her some time and time was all she needed to prepare her strongest spell.


She poured her power into her Sacred Gear, as a bluish orb of icy energy formed in between her palms, the more energy she poured the colder it got, by the time she had finished, the orb had turned from icy blue to pure white and the area around her was freezing too.


Augusta had also sensed this and had also finished taking care of the ice body doubles and hurriedly sent her hydra towards her.


The dozen flaming jaws of the Holy Flame hydra approached her but it was done, she had finished preparing her spell.


“Celcius Cross Trigger!”


And the world turned white.

“What do you mean you don’t know! Edward Chirstaldi took him away. He even had your letter and now you tell me that he just disappeared? How can he just disappear?” He heard Serafall shout at Gabriel and this time around it wasn’t even her just being childish due to her one sided rivalry with the Archangel but real anger.


The thing which they all had feared had happened.


Mathias and his peerage members were kidnapped.


While it wasn’t exactly verified what happened to them yet, he had received a signal from the device he had given to Shizuka and by the time they rushed there, they were already long gone.


And now, what could have turned into the first step to a possible Peace Treaty between Heaven and the Underworld, had turned into an emergency meeting to stop another Great War.


“Our people are trying to find out what happened to them. Even Edward has been missing since he went out to receive Mathias Buné Zagan and his peerage members. Please rest assured that we are doing our best to find them.” Gabriel answered calmly.


“I cannot rest assured when they just disappear not even thirty minutes after leaving with your people. Who knows what kind of situation he might be in right now?” Serafall argued.


“Please calm down, Serafall. We don’t know what happened to them.” Michael tried to speak and assure Serafall but it didn’t work either.


“If I may interrupt, Lord Michael.” He interrupted.


“Yes, Euclid.”


“I am almost certain that Mathias and his peerage members are in danger. One of them had a tracker and trigger which would let us know if they were in any danger. It was triggered from the place where they were teleported from. We have checked the area and there were signs of a spell prepared in advance there and it would have needed the car to stop there. There were no signs of any accidents or even resistance which meant that the car had stopped exactly at that point until the teleportation took place. All this doesn’t paint a good picture for us. I hope you will do whatever is needed from your side or… things will turn ugly.”


“Are you threatening us, devil!” Uriel, the ever hot head said in a loud voice.


“No, no I am not. We are beyond the point of threats. I am just stating the natural progression of things. We had already repeatedly warned your side about this so it shouldn’t come as a surprise. Should all this be a plot to harm Mathias… we go to war.”


“We are not going to war, Euclid.” Michael interrupted. “Heaven has nothing to do with this, I can swear this on my father’s name. All the three factions have lost a lot in the Great War. We do not wish for those tragedies to be repeated again.”


“Maybe you don’t… but can you say the same about your subordinates?” He questioned. “Can you say the same about the Church?” He asked and watched as a frown appeared on his face. “See, you can’t.”


Before he could speak however, they sensed two massive auras approaching towards them and looked in the direction to see a familiar figure appear with a cloaked person.


“Tiamat!” He spoke to the person in lead but the woman didn’t look like she was in any mood to talk.


“Where is Mathias?” She asked directly in a no nonsense tone. Not that Euclid would have tried to speak any nonsense after sensing the menacing aura around her.


“Mathias and his peerage members were teleported away on their way to the meeting location. We are still trying to find any signs of him.” he quickly explained the situation to her, not wanting to risk the ire of the angry dragoness.


He was glad that he did so because she turned her attention towards Michael who was taken aback by the intensity of the dragoness.


“Where is Mathias, Michael?” She asked once more.


“Tiamat. We really don’t know where Mathias and his three peerage members are. Believe me. We are trying our best to find him too.” He tried assuring her but by the look on her face, it didn’t look like it worked.


Tiamat just glared at the Archangel and spoke after a minute of glaring.


“Two hours. I will give you two hours. If Mathias doesn’t stand before me safe and sound by then… not even your father coming back from death will be able to save Heaven from my anger.”


Everyone present was taken aback by the blatant threat of the dragoness.


As expected, some didn’t take it so well.


“You will mind your tongue, dragon!” Uriel roared, holy flames coming to life around him.


Both Serafall and he took their stances against the angel too. Tiamat was currently on the same side as them. But before any of them could do anything, the cloaked figure moved at a pace beyond any of them, grabbing the furious Archangel’s face and smashing him into the ground, sending shockwaves around the entire area.


The flames burned the cloak and the shockwaves sent the burned cloak flying away to reveal the face under it, making them freeze.


“Your methods are too tame, Tiamat. These kinds of fools understand things better after a limb or two are removed from their bodies.”


It was Michael who broke out of his stupor first. “You are… The Crescent Circle Dragon, Crom Cruach?!”


While his mind was still reeling from the fact, trying to understand why the strongest of the Evil Dragons was suddenly here and asking about Mathias, even going so far as to pick up a fight with the entire Heaven, Tiamat’s aura flared.


The power of the strongest Dragon King came out in its fullest but to the surprise and horror of the Archangels, it didn’t just stop there but kept increasing.


Earth shook and the mountains started to crumble as her power reached a level which surpassed even Sirzechs and Ajuka’s, reaching into the realms of Heavenly Dragons.


He had only heard about the full power of the Heavenly Dragons from the older Devils, not having been born when they were sealed but if this was anything like the power they possessed, he could see why it would take all three factions to face the two of them… especially when they were outraged.


Tiamat’s show of power however didn’t just end there, her shadows expanded, and to everyone’s horror, claws erupted from the shadows as monstrous dragons stepped out of her shadow, climbing out.


That was… the Annihilation Maker.


How… just how and when did she get that?…


A Heavenly Dragon with a Longinus and that too one of the top four… just thinking about it sent shivers running down his spine. He could understand Michael’s state now.


“You have one hour fifty five minutes left, Michael. Find Mathias and pray to your departed father that he is unharmed or there will only be two factions left from now on.”


Unlike Crom’s angry roar, Tiamat’s calm voice scared Euclid even more.


Thankfully, right at that time, he received a message and breathed a sigh of relief.


“We have found their location.”


He said and immediately the magic power and the monsters evaporated and the two dragons turned towards him.


“Lead the way.”


He didn’t complain either. He himself was worried about Matty after all.


All of them took to the air and raced towards the location, each of them praying that nothing happens to Mathias… for their own reasons.

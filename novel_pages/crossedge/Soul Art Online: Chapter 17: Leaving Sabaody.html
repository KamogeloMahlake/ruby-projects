Rose nodded and I did too.


After all, it was my slip which caused this.


“In our team, apart from me, Elaine and Arthas are the only ones who know about the Supernatural world. I do plan to open up about us to the rest someday in the future though. Not anytime soon. So just keep this information to yourself.”


We talked about some more things and by that time, the rest also came out. The other three players we had rescued were badly shaken from the whole ordeal and were taking some time alone. We didn’t disturb them either. They needed some more time to get themselves up.

“Hey, a man can dream, right?” Star pouted.


Shiroe however looked at Bina. “Does it also give you navigation skills?” He asked after some thought and she nodded. “Yes. Basic knowledge.”


“Great. That could be very helpful with our quests. Anyways, since you are already a level 25 Scholar, we can help you level up instead of using the Mage Class Card. Still, I would suggest levelling the Pirate Class on the side.” He finished.


“What about you Rose?”


“I am… just Apprentice Warrior.” She answered, slightly dejectedly.


“Apprentice Warrior is good. We needed more frontline fighters. By the way, what kind of Class do you want to play going forward? A full warrior based class or do you want a hybrid Class with Magic mixed in?”


“A hybrid class… sounds better. I have experience…” She suddenly paused, realising her words.


“I see. So you have played Magic Classes in other games. Sure. We can search for a Hybrid Class.” Thankfully, Shiroe, having gone full planning mode, wasn’t paying any attention and handled the situation quite well here. “What kind of weapons do you use?” He asked.


“A Spear.”


“Hmm, the range of the spear is always welcome. We can check the Guild and Black Markets for other classes and see what route would be good for you. Anyways, we have a basic team now. All we need is to get everyone to the T4 Class next as our short term goal. We can add more members as time goes by.”


“How many people are we looking at as our final goal?” I asked.


“That’s hard to say. We don’t know how strong the final bosses will be. I mean just Tier 5 had that Rhino so who knows what we can face later on? Would the game even end at T10 or do we even have to face something beyond it?”


I winced a bit at the mention of that fucking Rhino…


“Let’s get back to the guild and get it levelled up fast so that we can add everyone to the guild first. Also, we will have to leave at least twenty places vacant for urgent invites from the next time.”


“Agreed.”

Bina was quiet most of the time and only spoke when questioned. Rose was also the same but in her case it wasn’t professionalism but rather shyness which prevented her from speaking out loud.

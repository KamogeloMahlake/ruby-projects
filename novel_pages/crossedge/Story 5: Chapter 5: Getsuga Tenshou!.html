What the heaven was she thinking?


She even had a smile on her face.

Tsubasa and Tsubaki stepped in front of her to guide her.

She had used harmless light spells, just making them look like magic bullets to trick Tsubaki into using her Mirror Alice and sending it into its cooldown time.


Tsubasa confronted her while Reya and Momo attacked her with spells.

Martial Arts?

The reason why she could rush so quickly was because there was some method she could use to disturb their magic and redirect their attacks.


Was it some kind of magic or artifact to disturb magic?


No.

Mastering such a technique would take a long time.


So this had to be something else.

“I just wish to challenge those stronger than me, Azazel. Your idea of peace doesn’t go well with this.”


The two other Satan descendants flew beside him.


“Since you have already decided on this, there is nothing to talk about.” Azazel brought out a golden dagger from his coat’s pocket. “Balance Breaker.”


“Downfall Dragon Another Armor.”


A flash of light later, he was covered in a golden scale mail similar to the White Dragon Emperor and flew up again.


Her sister and Archangel Gabriel joined the fight as well.


She almost instinctively tried to step in their direction only to be interrupted by a blast of magic in front of her. “Not now princess. You are not ready for that level of fight yet. I would be extremely sad if you were to be vaporised suddenly just by accidentally wandering close to those monsters.” She looked at the disguised Rias and realised what she was doing there.


The fight continued.


Vasco Strada swung his sword, releasing a wave of Holy Power towards them. The Leviathan and Asmodeus descendants flew away towards her sister and Gabriel while the White Dragon Emperor got ready to face them.


Tobio Ikuse also used his Sacred Gear to send a wave of black swords towards him and behind him Souji also joined.


Both attacks raced towards him, tearing up everything in front of it.


It was at this moment that a hole in space appeared directly in front of the White Dragon Emperor Vali.


Out of it came another familiar figure.


The guy with the mask. He held a sword in his hand. As the attacks came nearer, he swung his sword casually in a horizontal swing and Sona watched in awe as the attack which looked unstoppable to her….was cut into pieces and scattered into the air. The shockwave from the clash hit them near the outer parts of the battle field and she was thrown back several steps before she could barely stop with the help of barriers.


“You are late and that was my fight.” Vali Lucifer looked annoyed at the guy who merely scratched his head.


“Ahahahaha I opened a portal and saw an attack coming towards me. Scared the life out of me so I acted reflexively. Sorry about that.”


What bullshit. How was that a reflexive action?


“As for me being late. I was looking for the Hero faction. It seems those idiots tricked us. The barrier is locked but they are not participating in the fight. Seems like they want us to get beaten up. Scary!”


He remarked, almost childishly.

“Yes yes. Nice to meet you. My name is….Ichigo Kurosaki and I am gonna be the King of the Pirates….believe it dattebayo.”

Even Issei used the bracelet made by Azael to enter a Balance Breaker mode and join in.

The three took their positions around him but what came after was completely unexpected. It seemed as if he was dancing in between them, all their attacks passing just by him, never actually touching him even lightly.


All of those three were swordsmen whose skills were recognised by the whole world, said to rival even Gods. Two of them were even touted as the strongest humans alive and yet, they were being toyed around by one man without being able to put any kind of resistance at all.


From the start to the end, he used nothing but swordsmanship in the fight to take on all three of them.


- Vali Lucifer -


He kicked his so-called fated Rival, making him go crashing into the ground once more.

Yes.

One was someone who had faced and defeated even her ancestor while the other was someone who was said to have surpassed her ancestor. The same ancestor which she drivels about so much, her only claim to fame.

“You! Do you think this will end so easily? Do you think we will let it end just like that? Even if we die, we will make sure you all suffer too.” A black aura appeared around her.


Self Destruction?


But contrary to everyone’s expectations, she gathered the aura in her hand and she threw it….at Serafall’s little sister….who was fighting with….

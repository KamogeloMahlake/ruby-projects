I looked at the building in front of me. The place was old and in a remote alleyway in a town near Kyoto.


To be honest, it was a bad place for a thriving business but… there wasn’t much I could do.


I didn’t have the budget to get a better place.


And for my current situation, this place seemed to be quite good.


Thankfully it was well maintained.


If I advertised well, there was a chance I could catch the local crowd for my shop.


Right now my first priority is to hide from the yakuza.


I clenched my hands as I recalled those bastards.


They jumped on me the moment my parents passed away in the accident.


My property was taken away through forgery and bribing the officials.


But I was able to sell a large part of it away before they could get their hands on all of it.


The money I got from those along with the money in my parents' accounts was able to get me this place. A house with the ground floor as a restaurant and the upper two floors for living in this place in the whole other corner of the country.


And thus, I was here right out of High School, away from the home I grew up in, no family and friends to rely on and barely any money left in my pocket.


Almost eighteen years of my second life and this was the first time I felt alone.


I forced myself to get up, open the door and enter the building. I kept my bag on a table and looked around the place.


The Restaurant space was clean and honestly bigger than I was expecting. It had enough space to hold a dozen or so people quite comfortably.


It would still need some work before I can start work here.


My Father used to run a very successful Restaurant in the Tokyo area and my home was far better than this but that was what had attracted the Yakuza.


I don’t need to think about those right now. I shook my head and focused on the work in front of me.


Over the next two hours, I cleaned the place and rearranged the furniture according to my own choice.


The day was still young so I took the opportunity to go out and buy some ingredients for my Restaurant.


While doing so I inquired more about the nearby area and also spread a word about my restaurant.


I returned back home and decided to test the kitchen while I cooked myself a meal.


It was a simple meal. Just a bowl of Udon noodles and soup.


“Happy Birthday to me, I guess.” I sighed.


And that’s when the change happened.


A virtual screen appeared in front of me.


I must be too tired.


Shaking my head, I sat down to eat.


The funny thing is that I didn't stop seeing the screen in front of me the entire time.


Am I really going mad?


I looked at the screen and it only contained a few lines.


Restaurant Level: 1

Cooking Level: 1

Restaurant Privilege: Level 1


Store

Inventory


Just to test things out, I thought of opening the Store Option.


And just like that another menu appeared in front of me again.


Within the menu there were hundreds if not thousands of food items.


Beside each item, there was a price and the currency changed with my thought.


… this was too detailed to be a dream.


Only one way to check it.


I went down the list and saw a chicken, around twelve times more expensive than the one I bought today. I didn’t have anything to lose anyway so I clicked on the purchase button.


….


….


Nothing happened.


I guess stress is getting to me.


Yeah. Getting a fantasy type cheat in my reincarnated life… What is this, one of those anime worlds?


I ate the food and went back to the kitchen to clean up. When I finished, I decided to prepare for tomorrow’s shop opening.


And so I opened the refrigerator and suddenly paused at the sight in front of me.


There lay a large piece of whole chicken in the Refrigerator… which looked exactly like the one I had just purchased.


With shaking hands I picked up the thing and observed. It was different from the normal chicken.


No.


This cannot be real.


I quickly tried to verify it.


The screen was still in front of me so I purchased another thing.


With my heart beating fast, I opened the refrigerator once more and sure enough, there in front of me was another product all of a sudden.


… fuck.


I experimented with some other things and sure enough… it worked.


Suddenly another thought struck me.


Money.


If the products are real, is the money being charged for it also real? To verify it, I quickly ran to my cash counter and opened it quickly.


Sure enough, there was money missing from the place.


I did a quick maths and the amount missing was equal to the money I had used for the products.


Tired, I sat down on a chair.


Everything was real.


I really had a new power.


Or was it the power of this place?


I didn’t know.


Over the rest of the night, I explored everything I could about the System.


Doing so, I figured out a few things about this power.


First, it didn’t tell me almost anything due to low accessibility.


There was a note saying ‘Figure it out yourself.’


Second, everything I order comes from a place called Mathias’ Farm.


Now, I had no idea who this Mathias was or where he lived but damn this guy could produce some fine crops. Each and everything from his farm was way beyond anything found in the nearby markets.


Starting from common vegetables to food which supposedly increased someone’s health and vitality.


The only problem was the price it took to buy those things.


When I checked the list, it extended from simple, daily use ingredients to insane things with just as insane prices.


Finally, my cooking had improved beyond anything I had ever known.


It was even better than my dad who was a professional chef for all his life.


With all these, I finally felt a semblance of support in this world once more after all the shit I had been through these last few months.


Let’s hope that my new life in this place can be a quiet and peaceful one.

“Would you like to join my family?” He asked as he offered his hand. “You can be my grandson and Miu’s little brother if you agree.”


“B-b-but why? People… they don’t like me. I am…”


“Then they are blind. I don’t care about what people do. I just wish to ask for your opinion. Normally, children can be adopted from the Orphanage but since you are not living in one, then I can directly bring you with me.”


“Yes yes. See, we even look similar. Blonde hair and blue eyes. We totally look like siblings.” Said Miu happily


The boy got tears in his eyes and tried to speak but his voice choked when he tried to speak any words.


I patted his head. “You can take your time. Getting a loving family is something….very few are blessed with. Hayato-san is a very great man. He will always take care of you.”


He looked at me and then back at Hayato-san and nodded.


“I… thank you. I would love to join your family, Jii-san.”


“Ahahahaha Silas-kun. I just got a grandson today. We are having a party.”


I smiled and gave them a 50% discount on everything.


The crowd welcomed the newest addition to our little group with great enthusiasm.


Meanwhile, the purple haired girl looked at everything in wonder.


I prepared her order first as she was also invited by the other patrons to join the party. She refused politely but kept looking at them in a daze.


She did ask about the Shinobi thing and found out about the powers of the Restaurant too.


“Is it real? Can people from other worlds really visit this place?”


“Yes. You might not realise it yet but you probably are from a different world from us as well. This shop is actually in a town bordering Kyoto.”


“Kyoto? That’s… quite far away.”


“Oh. Which place are you from?”


“I….I am from Fuyuki City.”


“Fuyuki City? I am sorry but I haven’t heard of such a place.” I replied.


“It’s near Kobe.”


“Oh. You can still get back home whenever you want to.”


I walked to the door and opened it, showing her a different scene. “This is my world. Similarly, when you open the door, you can get to your world.”


“That’s…”


“Amazing right? I know. We still have a hard time believing it.”


She nodded lightly.


“By the way, your order should be ready. Are you sure you don’t want to try something?”


She shook lightly as if suddenly awakened. “Umm thank you but I… am getting late.”


“Alright. Come back again, miss?”


“Sakura.” She answered.


“Miss Sakura. My name is Silas Evans. Please visit us again.”


She left the Restaurant but not before looking back at everyone once more.


– Satoru Gojo –


He watched the fight between Aoi and Yuji against the curse which had almost taken out Nanami and Nobara from a distance.


Both of them had lived thanks to him reaching there in time and using the reverse curse technique.


"Sensei, shouldn't we help them?" Nobara asked, looking worried.


He didn't answer and just looked in the direction. It took him only a fraction of a second to realise just how badly he would have fucked up if he didn't encounter the Restaurant and the guy Kamijo Touma inside when he had.


He took two weeks to verify that none of it was a trap from the one occupying Suguru's body.


The owner also made him cautious but when he did, he took the help of the so-called Esper and to his surprise, the boy was able to bypass his Limitless and touch him as well as dispell the Reversal Red Technique.


It was a scary technique and yet a boon at the same time.


One touch could exorcise the worst of the curses permanently.


Those bastards at the top would have done anything to get control of him or even try to make him into a cursed puppet to control his power if possible and in doing so, most likely causing the ruin of the entire Jujutsu world.


As strong as his power was, Satoru had observed that he couldn’t even cause ripples in the Restaurant or the owner.


Thinking about it, a smile came to his lips.


Other worlds.


‘Are you the strongest because you're Satoru Gojo? Or are you Satoru Gojo because you're the strongest?’


It seems we would know the answer.


The great food was just a cherry on the cake.


On observation of Kamijo Touma’s power, he found that any technique or supernatural event was simply erased when it came in contact with his right hand and that’s why he asked for a handshake from across the door, while in the Prison Realm. Sure enough, as soon as his hand came in contact with the Prison Realm, the entire thing crashed.


And thus, here he was… free.


The imposter had been able to flee in the time it took him to talk to Touma back in the Restaurant but all his lackeys were still out. He was able to arrive right on time and barely save the lives of both Nanami and Kugisaki.


And this time, Satoru decided to see things through.


Since there was such an interesting thing in front of him, he didn’t need any distractions.


The unregistered Special Grade Curses, those bastard higher ups, the imposter and Sukuna… all of them need to step aside.


He was pretty sure they would try to put the blame of the whole incident on him so… he would let them.


Since they think Satoru Gojo is sealed or possibly dead… let them.


And when they get out of their way, ready to dig their graves….


Maybe in doing so, both little Megumi and Yuji would progress further… just like now.


“After today, we are increasing the training of the team, especially yours. Megumi and Yuji will have to train on their own for a while.”


“What exactly are you planning to do now?” Nanami asked, suspiciously.


“To finish things.”


- Hiruzen Sarutobi -


“What do you mean you can’t find Naruto? He’s an eight year old. How could he hide from all of you?” He shouted as the Anbu in front of him kneeled.


“We don’t know, Sir. One moment he was in front of us and the next he was gone. No matter how much we tried to find him, there was no trace of him in the area.”


He narrowed his eyes at the statement.


Several options jumped through his mind at the same time.


Genjutsu.


Space Time Ninjutsu.


But who?


“Tell me everything about the incident.”


The Anbu explained and he was dumbstruck at the explanation.


The only explanation was that someone put them under a Genjutsu and disappeared with Naruto.


In recent years, the village hasn't been in the best state due to the events of the past few years.


The Third Shinobi war, the Nine Tails war, Orochimaru’s defection, the Uchiha situation and now this.


He had the Anbu sent to the T&I department to have him checked but he doubted there would be anything in him. These were the people he had personally selected.


Kakashi could have been used to keep an eye on Naruto but the village couldn’t afford such a talent guarding someone and not going out on missions.


Shisui was a good option but…


Itachi needed to be used to keep an eye on the Uchiha situation.


Jiraya was always out maintaining his spy network while the less said about Tsunade, the better.


He had thought that with Naruto going to the academy, the guard on him could be relaxed as he would be in the presence of the teachers of the academy for most of the day. There Iruka could probably get close to him, forming a bond between him and the village. But Iruka still hadn’t opened up to him.


And then… this happens suddenly.


Who could it be?


Other villages?


The Uchiha?


Or maybe… his old friend was up to his antics once more?


He shook his head to clear his thoughts as he sent out a summon for the Inuzuka and the Aburame Clans.


All his Anbu were already scouring the village for any signs of Naruto.


In a short while several members of the village were in his office.


Shibi Aburame and several of the Inuzuka Jonins were present. Kakashi, Itachi and Tsume as well as her daughter were out for missions so they couldn’t be here.


His two advisors and Danzo also came in just in time.


“Hiruzen, how can you be so careless?” Koharu commented but one look from him shut her up.


He was not in a mood to joke around at this time.


Naruto was a sensitive topic for him due to several reasons.


“What is the case, Hokage-sama?” Shibi asked.


He let out a sigh.


“This information needs to be kept under wraps so I put it under an A Ranked secret. If you do not wish to participate after hearing this, you can walk away.”


None of them did so he continued.


“As of eight hours ago, Naruto Uzumaki has gone missing.” He stated and sure enough there was a cacophony of noise but he stopped them with one signal.


“We can stay here all day arguing about things but that will come after we find him. Our first priority should be to find out where he is.”


They quieted down and he ordered.


“Shibi, you take your clan’s selected people and search the area of the forests around Konoha. The Inuzuka clan will search throughout the village for any clues. Inform me personally if you find anything.”


They bowed and left the room, leaving only him and his previous roommates behind.


“It must be those Uchiha. Hiruzen, your kindness has finally left them to show their true face. Do you think the village can resist another Nine Tails attack? Give me permission and I will…” Danzo began.


“You will do nothing.” He cut him off in the middle of the sentence, his voice firm.


“But Danzo has a point. Those damned Uchiha have truly crossed the line.” Koharu started speaking but was cut off by his raised hand.


“First of all, we don’t know if it was the Uchiha. Pushing them at such a point would cut off any chance of peace. Second, if they do have Naruto, it will be even more dangerous to proceed without caution. If the Nine Tails is released, there won’t be a fourth Hokage to stop him this time. You all… would just run aside and hide like the last time.”


“Hiruzen!”


“Am I wrong? Where were you when the village was attacked the last time? Trying to stop the Uchiha? You went behind everyone’s back, out of your authority to stop the clan. If the Uchiha had really been behind the Nine Tails attack, they wouldn’t have stopped so easily at your command. But they did. They even accepted moving to the outskirts of the village under surveillance for the sake of peace. This… no one can deny. On the other hand you, Danzo, have tried to assassinate me twice.” He replied, shutting him up suddenly.


“If we consider the crimes and who to punish, I should have executed you years ago.”


“Hiruzen, calm down.” Homura tried to mediate but he shut him up too.


“The Uchiha might be responsible for this. I am not denying it. But what matters the most is Naruto’s safety. I will not have your doubts endanger any of it. If the Uchiha are responsible for it, then they will be dealt with in the same manner as well.”


“You are letting your feelings for the Jinchuriki get in the way of your judgement.” Danzo bit back.


“All of you may leave. Oh and Danzo, the Root is not to move out of their base till this matter is taken care of.”


“Hiruzen, are you…”


“You haven’t left me much of an option. Do you think I have no idea about Shisui?”


He observed the expressions of his friend while his reaction would have been the textbook definition of innocent, he knew Danzo more than that. That expression was more than proof that Danzo was responsible for Shisui’s disappearance.


If so then with Shisui’s Mangekyou Sharingan, he would have more than enough of a tool to brainwash Naruto.


“You are also to remain in the Hokage guesthouse under the Anbu surveillance until Naruto is found. Everyone will be informed that you are under a temporary leave due to ill health.”


“Hiruzen, you are too much.”


“I'm Hokage.”


All of them left the room, leaving him alone in his thoughts.


He looked at his successor’s picture staring back at him and felt a pang in his heart.


They owed Naruto too much and it pained him greatly that he couldn’t give him a better life. He had only assured himself that it was for the best of the village and for his own protection but even he knew how hollow that reason sounded.


To be honest, he was tired and after Biwako’s death… he had lost some of his will too.


But whatever the reason may be, he will be damned if he let that become a reason for endangering the boy.


If that required the ‘God of Shinobi’ to come back, then so be it.
